Bogx - Guided Tour Service - Use Case

Authors: Hrimiuc Daniel-Marin and Chirila Sabin

Date: February 3, 2024

Imagine a scenario where a visitor to the Botanical Garden of Iasi desires to explore specific zones within the garden. Whether they're new to the area or simply seeking to discover new zones, navigating the vast garden can be daunting without proper guidance. Rather than opting for a traditional guided tour, which may involve hiring a guide, we propose a more accessible solution for both visitors and the Botanical Garden itself: our guided tour service.

Here's how it works:

  1. Zone Selection: The user begins by selecting the zones they wish to explore from a list of options provided. This intuitive process allows users to tailor their experience according to their preferences and interests
  2. Guided Navigation: Utilizing our advanced algorithm, the user is seamlessly redirected to a dedicated page containing detailed instructions on navigating through the botanical garden. These instructions not only outline the precise directions to follow but also include accompanying images sourced from the garden's map, enhancing clarity and ease of navigation.

Through this streamlined approach, visitors can embark on personalized explorations of the Botanical Garden of Iasi, discovering its diverse zones and attractions with confidence and ease.
